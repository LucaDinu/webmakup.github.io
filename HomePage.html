Tips for a wonderful day in Zaandam

Introduction about Zaandam

Zaandam is a city in the province of North Holland at a distance of about 20 km from the capital of The Netherlands, Amsterdam.

From the moment you get off the train, you are surprised by the special architecture of Inntel Hotel and Town Hall, whose architecture is inspired by traditional Zaan houses, and the beauty of the pedestrian zone.

Despite the architecture, this city is well-known for Zaanse Schans, a natural, open-air museum with hundreds of thousands of visitors every year.
